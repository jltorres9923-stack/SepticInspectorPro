Septic Inspector Pro is a field toolkit for septic inspectors and installers. It replaces the clipboard, the sizing chart taped to the truck door, and the evening spent formatting a report.

Calculators

Tank sizing from bedroom count with design flow shown at every step, and drain field sizing from perc rate — absorption area, trench linear feet, and a suggested trench layout. Typical reference values with clear disclaimers: local code always governs.

Site Inspections

A 25-point checklist across six sections, ordered the way you actually walk a property: records and access, tank, distribution box, drain field, setbacks, site conditions. Tap to mark OK, Concern, Fail, or N/A. Add notes and camera photos to any item.

Branded PDF Reports

Generate a professional inspection report on site: your company name and license on the cover, overall result, system summary, complete findings, and a labeled photo appendix. Share it before you leave the driveway.

One-time purchase, no subscription. The free version includes every calculator and up to three saved inspections with sample reports. A single lifetime unlock removes limits and watermarks. All data stays on your device.
